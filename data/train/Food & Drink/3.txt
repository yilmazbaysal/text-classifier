Burger King Is Introducing A Froot Loops Shake

To the delight of cereal lovers across the country, Burger King just announced that they will start spinning Froot Loop Shakes on April 17.

Made with vanilla soft serve, Froot Loops cereal pieces and a special "sweet sauce" this new cereal milkshake will join the classic chocolate, vanilla and strawberry shakes as well as the Oreo shake already on BK's dessert menu for a limited time.

Excited to get your hands on this delicious breakfast-dessert hybrid? Froot Loops Shakes will be sold at Burger Kings nationwide for $2.99.
