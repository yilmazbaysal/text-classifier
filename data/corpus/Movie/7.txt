Beauty and the Beast tops $400M domestically and $900M globally

Yesterday saw Beauty and the Beast become only the 26th movie in history to cross the $400 million threshold domestically. Combined with the international performance to date, Beauty and the Beast has also now hit the $900 million global mark. Domestically, the film has earned $401.1 million and internationally it has grossed $498.9 million.
